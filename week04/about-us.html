Our Story

The Abuja Community Garden Initiative sprouted from a shared dream of bringing nature closer to our urban lives. Founded in early 2022 by local enthusiasts, we've grown into a vibrant community transforming concrete spaces into lush gardens.

Our Vision

We envision a city where green spaces are abundant, communities thrive together, and everyone has access to the benefits of nature. Through education and participation, we aim to sow seeds of change that will flourish for generations.
