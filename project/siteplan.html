EcoTravel Adventures - Site Plan

Site Name

EcoTravel Adventures

This website promotes **eco-friendly travel** by offering guides, destination reviews, and sustainability tips for travelers.

Potential Domain Name: www.ecotraveladventures.com (Check availability)

Site Purpose

The goal of this website is to educate and inspire people to travel **sustainably** by providing:

  • Eco-friendly travel guides
  • Tips on reducing carbon footprints while traveling
  • A curated list of green-certified hotels & destinations
  • A forum where travelers can share experiences

Scenarios

Visitors might ask:

  • What are the **best eco-friendly hotels** in Europe?
  • How can I reduce **plastic waste** while traveling?
  • Are there **sustainable travel tours** available?
  • What destinations have the **best renewable energy policies**?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Green (#2E8B57) - Used for headings, buttons, and important elements.
  • Beige (#F5F5DC) - Background color for a natural and earthy feel.
  • White (#FFFFFF) - Used for content areas for contrast and readability.
  • Dark Gray (#333333) - Used for body text.

Typography

  • Josefin Sans - Used for all heading.
  • Teko - Used for some heading.
  • Port Lligat Sans - Used for some text.
  • Roboto - Used for some text.
